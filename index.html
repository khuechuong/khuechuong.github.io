Who am I ?

A Roboticist / Computer Scientist Located In Our Lovely Earth

I am a third-year PhD student in Advance Robotic and Automation (ARA) Lab at the University of Nevada, Reno advised by Prof. Hung (Jim) La. My research focuses on autonomous infrastructure inspection robots.

Prior to pursuing my PhD, I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science at the University of Oklahoma. I was with ARA Lab under their REU program as an undergraduate student and worked on multi-robotic systems and hybrid steel inspection robot.
